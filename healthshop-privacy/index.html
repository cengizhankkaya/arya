HealthShop App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

HealthShop app ("App", "We", "Our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how HealthShop app collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal data.

By using this app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

During app usage, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, first name, last name
  • Authentication: Secure login credentials through Firebase Authentication
  • User Preferences: In-app settings and preferences

2.2 Device Information

  • Device Details: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • App Usage: In-app interactions, feature usage, error reports

2.3 Media Content

  • Photos: Product photos (through camera or gallery access)
  • QR Code/Barcode Data: Scanned product barcodes

3. Data Deletion and User Rights

3.1 Right to Delete Account and Data

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data. To request account deletion:

  • Contact us via email: privacy@healthshop.com
  • Use the in-app settings to delete your account
  • We will process your request within 30 days

3.2 What Gets Deleted

  • Your account information (email, name)
  • All product data you've added
  • Photos you've uploaded
  • Usage analytics data

4. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions and data deletion requests:

Email: privacy@healthshop.com

Address: Izmir, Turkey

Response Time: Within 30 days

5. Legal Basis

This privacy policy is prepared in accordance with:

  • Turkey: Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK)
  • European Union: General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Google Play Store: Privacy Policy Requirements

By accepting this privacy policy, you can start using the HealthShop app.
